Work

What I do best is work with cross-functional groups to accomplish great things.

Delighting the world's iconic brands with outstanding design and user experience

From leading the creative sessions at kickoff through making sure a project is delivered on time, I’m obsessed with creating the best branded digital experiences. My number-one priority is to ensure that the creative group exceeds the client's expectations when it comes to all things design, front-end, and user experience related.

Custom front-end framework increases conversion by 3% and organic traffic by 100%

Long before most online retailers had moved to modern responsive design, I was there.

With help from a talented group of web designers and front-end developers, we built a custom front-end framework for the BrandShop commerce platform. The new framework allowed us so much more control – it was amazing!

I led and contributed to the complete rebuild of all page elements, creating a user experience that increased conversion and doubled our organic traffic.

Taking the Complex and Making it Simple

When faced with the challenge of converting Batteries.com and its thousands of pages from the IBM WebSphere to the BrandShop commerce platform, I had to start at the very core.

Using an Excel file of the data, I helped devise a strategy to dynamically generate all of the pages needed for redirects and to retain valuable SEO content.

From there, I lead an agile, cross-functional team, in which I helped guide the design, user interfaces, and user experience with whiteboard sketches, sitemaps, wireframes, and mockups.

The final product is a site that not only looks outstanding but makes the shopping process simple.
